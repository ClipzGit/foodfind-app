About us

"At FoodFind we want to make sure ingredients in peoples homes aren't going to go to waste. So therefore we have created a system that allows anybody to find recipes based on the ingredients that you have in your kitchen. We match all our users with the highest quality recipes that have been sourced from trusted food cooking websites out there."
